About

My current research focus is the numerical investigation of granular materials with Prof. Corey O'Hern at Yale University.

Prior to joining O'Hern group, I worked for Prof. Alessandro Gomez (Yale) on turbulent combustion for two years.

I did my master's at USC under the supervision of Prof. P. D. Ronney on edgeflame propagation.

I was previously an undergraduate exchange student at Tohoku University working on microgravity combustion with Prof. Kaoru Maruta.
